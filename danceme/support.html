Common Issues

Generation is taking a long time

Generation runs on remote servers and processing time can vary by demand and input complexity. Check the video status in the app's history screen.

Generation failed

Failed generations may be refunded as credits automatically based on service outcome. If your credit balance does not update correctly, contact support.

Photo does not work well

For best results, use a clear, well-lit photo with visible faces and avoid heavy blur.

Purchases and Credits

DanceMe sells consumable credits for AI video generation through Apple In-App Purchase.

  • Payments are processed by Apple
  • DanceMe does not store your payment card information
  • Credits are consumable and used per generation

If you were charged by Apple and have a billing issue, you can contact Apple through Report a Problem.

If the issue is a credit balance mismatch inside the app, contact DanceMe support using the email above.
