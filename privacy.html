Privacy Policy

App: Add Text  •  Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Add Text (“the app”) handles user information.

Information We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or share personal data.

  • No accounts are required
  • No analytics are used
  • No tracking technologies are used
  • No data is transmitted to external servers

All editing happens entirely on your device.

Photos and Videos

The app accesses your photos or videos only when you choose to import media for editing. Edited photos or videos are saved to your photo library only when you export them.

The app does not upload or store your media.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove a watermark from exports. All purchases are processed securely by Apple using your Apple ID. The app does not receive or store any payment information.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect personal information and is safe to use by users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
