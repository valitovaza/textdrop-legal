Common Issues

Generation is taking a long time

Generation runs on remote servers, so processing time can vary. Song generation and video generation have different processing times. You can check the current status in the app's projects or history screen.

Generation failed

Failed generations may be refunded as credits based on service outcome and app logic. If your credit balance does not update correctly, contact support.

Song result does not match my prompt

For better results, use a clear song description, choose a focused style, and add lyrics when you want more control over vocals. Very broad or conflicting prompts may produce less predictable results.

I cannot create a video clip

Video generation requires a completed song, a selected song excerpt, and an image for the video step. Make sure the song has finished processing and any required media permissions are enabled.

I cannot upload an image

Make sure SongClip has access to your camera or photo library in iOS Settings. If permission was denied earlier, you may need to re-enable it manually.

Push Notification Help

Not receiving notifications?

  • Check iOS Settings > Notifications > SongClip and make sure notifications are enabled
  • Make sure Focus mode, Low Power Mode, or notification settings are not suppressing alerts
  • Confirm internet connection and try reopening the app once

You can still view generation status in your projects or history even if notifications are disabled.

If notifications still do not arrive, contact support at valitov.azamat.m@gmail.com.
