Privacy Policy

App: CosplayAI  |  Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CosplayAI ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app.

CosplayAI creates AI-generated cosplay images from a portrait photo and an anime reference image. Generation is processed on remote servers and may take time to complete.

Information We Collect

We collect information needed to operate the app, process image generation, deliver results, and improve reliability.

  • Photos you upload, including your portrait photo and anime reference image used for generation
  • Generated image outputs while processing and delivering results
  • Anonymous app or install identifiers stored securely on your device and used for app functionality such as continuity, credits, and generation-related records
  • Authentication-related identifiers, including anonymous or technical account identifiers used to operate backend services
  • App usage analytics (for example, via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash and diagnostic data (for example, via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Purchase-related events for consumable credits, but not your full payment card data
  • Push notification tokens if you allow notifications

Anonymous Identifier and Backend Access

CosplayAI may generate and store an anonymous app install identifier on your device to support core functionality.

We may also use backend authentication or technical account identifiers, including anonymous authentication, to securely operate app services such as generation requests, credits, history, and result delivery.

These identifiers are used for app functionality and service continuity. They are not used for cross-app or cross-website advertising tracking.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Process AI image generation requests you start
  • Upload your selected images and deliver generated results
  • Maintain app functionality such as credits, history, and generation-related records
  • Operate and improve app performance, stability, and reliability
  • Detect crashes, bugs, fraud, abuse, and service issues
  • Support consumable credit purchases and credit reconciliation for failed generations when applicable
  • Send optional notifications such as generation completion or failure updates

Photos and AI Image Generation

When you select a portrait photo and an anime reference image and start generation, those images are transmitted to our backend infrastructure and service providers to process your request.

Generated outputs and related metadata may also be stored or processed to complete generation, support your history, and deliver the result to your device.

CosplayAI is not intended to function as an unlimited permanent cloud gallery. However, generation records, source files, and outputs may be retained for a period of time as needed to operate the app, support history, investigate failures, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate the app, including infrastructure, analytics, crash reporting, storage, messaging, and payment processing.

  • Firebase Authentication for backend access and service identity
  • Firebase Firestore for generation-related records and app data
  • Firebase Storage for uploaded media and generated outputs
  • Firebase Analytics for usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification Services for optional notifications
  • Cloud processing providers used to generate AI images and deliver results
  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchases for consumable credit purchases

These providers may process technical information necessary to provide their services, such as device information, app version, request metadata, diagnostic data, and identifiers needed for delivery or security.

Tracking and Advertising

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs and do not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Any identifiers used by CosplayAI are used for app functionality, service operation, security, analytics, diagnostics, and result delivery, not for cross-app advertising tracking.

In-App Purchases (Consumable Credits)

CosplayAI offers consumable credit purchases. Purchases are processed securely by Apple using your Apple ID.

We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

We may receive transaction-related information needed to validate purchases, grant credits, prevent fraud, and support customer issues.

Push Notifications

If you allow notifications, CosplayAI may collect and process push notification identifiers, such as APNs and/or Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens, to send app notifications.

  • Generation completed
  • Generation failed
  • Important service-related alerts

Notification identifiers may be associated with your app instance or backend identity so notifications can be delivered to the correct device.

You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed to operate the app, complete requested processing, maintain security, support history and purchases, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Uploaded images and generated outputs may be retained temporarily or for a limited operational period depending on app functionality, storage practices, and service needs.

Analytics and crash diagnostics may be retained by our service providers according to their own settings and policies.

Your Choices

  • You can manage camera, photo library, and notification permissions in iOS Settings
  • You can stop using the app at any time
  • You can contact us with privacy questions or requests using the email below

If you want to make a privacy-related request, contact us and we will review your request in accordance with applicable law.

Security
